Art is a quest.

“I found I could say things with colour and shapes that I couldn't say any other way — things I had no words for.”

My work sits at the intersection of craft and concept, exploring form, material, and narrative through contemporary visual language.

The name Simsiqueste suggests both a journey and a question—a quest for meaning through visual expression. Every piece I create is chosen with that intention.
